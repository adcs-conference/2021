Registration

ADCS is free but we need you to register so we know how many to cater for, and so we're aware of any special requirements or requests. Please register by 28 November.

Here's the online registration form

Location

ADCS 2019 is being held at the University of Technology Sydney, Australia. UTS is right in the CBD, very close to Central station.

Look for the Faculty of Engineering and IT building, 81 Broadway. The building has two entrances, one at the corner of Broadway and Jones St, and the other at the corner of Broadway and Wattle St. We'll be in room CB11.00.405. Registration will be in the "student lounge" (foyer).

Dinner

On the evening of Thursday 5th you are invited to join ADCS and ALTA attendees for the symposium dinner. We will have a private room at The Eight, which specialises in Chinese seafood but can accommodate other requirements. Dinner is at your own cost ($52/head, wine and beer can be bought on the spot).

If you'd like to join us (and we hope you can), please indicate this on the registration form.
