Information for Presenters


Full Papers

Full paper presentations are allocated 20 minutes + 5 minutes for Q&A.

In addition, long papers can also be presented as a poster during the Poster Session.

Short Papers

Short paper presentations are allocated 10 minutes + 5 minutes for Q&A.

In addition, short papers must also be given as a poster during the Poster Session.

Unrefereed Posters

This year all conference attendees are invited (but not required) to present a poster during a poster session. The poster session is designed to provide feedback on research in progress and we expect it to be most useful for PhD students to get early feedback on their research direction.

Lecture presenting system

Presenters can use their own laptop computer to plug to the multimedia system (through VGA or HDMI) or use the resident computer. Please ensure your presentation is working on the multimedia system in the break before your session; take the chance also to introduce yourself to the person chairing your session.

Poster setup

Phyiscal posters should be A1 in size and portrait orientation to support the space available.
